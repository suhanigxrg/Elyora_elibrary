Frequently Asked Questions

How do I search for books? +
Use the search bar at the top of the page. You can search by book title, author name, or tags. You can also filter by category using the dropdown next to the search bar.
How do I add books to my favorites? +
Click the heart icon on any book card or in the book details modal. Favorited books will appear in your Favorites section, accessible from the sidebar.
How do I download books for offline reading? +
Click the download button on any book card or in the book details modal. Downloaded books will be available in your Downloads section for offline access.
How does the Continue Reading feature work? +
When you start reading a book, your progress is automatically saved. Books with reading progress will appear in the Continue Reading section on the home page.
How do I use the reader controls?+
Adjust font size, switch themes, search within the book, and navigate between chapters. Settings are auto-saved.
How do I highlight text and add notes? +
Select text while reading to highlight it. Use the Notes button in the left sidebar to add notes.
Can I customize the reading experience?+
Adjust font size, line height, switch between light and dark themes, and access various tools like TOC, highlights, and notes.
How do I navigate between categories?+
Use the dropdown in the search bar, category tiles on the home page, or browse category pages.
