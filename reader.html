Chapter 2: Ancient Whispers

The book she found in the lighthouse attic was bound in midnight blue leather, its pages filled with symbols that seemed to shimmer in the candlelight...

As Sarah traced her finger along the ancient text, she felt a strange warmth emanating from the pages. The symbols began to glow with an ethereal light, and whispers of forgotten languages filled the air around her.

She had always been drawn to mysteries, but this was unlike anything she had ever encountered. The lighthouse keeper had warned her about the attic, speaking in hushed tones about things that were better left undisturbed.

But curiosity had always been her weakness, and now she found herself deep in the heart of something far greater than she could have imagined. The book pulsed with energy, as if it were alive, waiting for someone worthy to unlock its secrets.
